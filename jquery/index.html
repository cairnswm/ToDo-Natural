  • What is it?

    A 4,500km mountain bike cycle through the North American Rocky Mountains.

    Are you crazy?

    How long will it take?

    I'll be away from home for 44 days, of which 28 days are planned for the ride. I have 7 days spare in case I dont finish in time.
